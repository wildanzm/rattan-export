About Us

We started operations in 2021 as professional company in Majalengka City, Indonesia. In addition, we have started to work in the furniture, fruits, spices and pharmacy sector, whose products are taken directly from many farmers in rural areas, which are standardized for the export market. We work with farmers and furniture craftsmen. The company's vision is to meet common needs and to improve the welfare of farmers by maintaining the sustainability of product stocks.
